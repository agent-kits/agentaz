# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

AgentKits blueprints are design-time references; they ship no servers, no
credentials, and execute nothing on our infrastructure. If you find a security
issue in the registry, the AgentAz™ schema, or a published blueprint, please
report it privately:

- Email: **hello@agent-kits.com** (subject: `SECURITY`)
- Please do not open a public issue for undisclosed vulnerabilities.

We aim to acknowledge reports within 5 business days.

## Scope

In scope: the AgentKits website/source, the AgentAz™ JSON Schema, and blueprint
content. Out of scope: third-party frameworks a blueprint references, and any
runtime system you build from a blueprint.
